Documentation for MB3

This agent is used for scheduling and controlling the Ricoh MB-3 Media Board.

Notes on the MB3

Touchscreen
The touch screen currently works. Tapping emulates a mouse click (accompanied by a beep).
Mouse
There is a normal mouse attached to the computer (open the top drawer). A Gyropoint mouse is available and can be hooked up. To use that mouse, plug it in where the current mouse is plugged in, then reboot, turn on the transmitter (on belt clip) the green light should be on. Press the but on the bottom of the unit (the "activate" button) once to activate the mouse (should hear the gyros spin up). Now simply tilt your hand left/right or up/down to move the mouse. The buttons on top work like normal mouse buttons. The mouse has a second mode which requires you to hold down the activate to register movement -- to get out of that mode, simply click the activate button. Please turn off the transmitter when done.
Screen
The screen operates at 640x480 resolution.
DVD
The DVD drive will read digital video disks or normal CD's. It will NOT read CD-R disks (CD-RW disks are OK.)
