The PIA group

The PIA group was officially formed in January 1997 after months of contemplating key technologies for future office environments.

PIA stands for (among other things) Platforms for Information Appliances. We believe that office machines of the future will be simple, task based devices (appliances) for manipulating paper and electronic documents (information).

Our charter: Develop a framework or platform for building Information Appliances.

Our customers: Product groups can use our framework in two ways:

  1. for rapid prototyping of product concepts
  2. as the software design and specification

Our technology: Consists of three pieces,

  1. a core Agency for sending and receiving electronic documents and managing the electronic "agents" which process those document,
  2. an html based language for instructing agents, and
  3. an agent development kit facilitating the creation of domain specific agents

Members: Steve Savitzky,Rithy Roth, and Greg Wolff
