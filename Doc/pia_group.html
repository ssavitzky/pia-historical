The PIA group

The PIA group was officially formed in January 1997 after many months of contemplating key technologies for future office environments.

PIA stands for (among other things) Platforms for Information Appliances. We believe that the office machines of the future will be simple, task based devices (appliances) for manipulating paper and electronic documents (information).
Our charter: Design and build the key technologies which makes Information Appliances possible and powerful.
Our customers: We work with product groups to implement our framework on their devices.
Our technology: Consists of three pieces,

  1. a Core for sending and receiving electronic documents and managing the electronic "agents" which process those document,
  2. an html based language for instructing agents, and
  3. an agent development kit facilitating the creation of domain specific agents
