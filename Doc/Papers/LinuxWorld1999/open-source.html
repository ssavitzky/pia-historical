Open Source

Why Open Source?

  • Ideal for small offices and workgroups
    • (our target market)
    • Small businesses don't have lots of money for software
    • A small group can't afford to hire experts
    • Eric Raymond's ``widget frosting'' business model
  • Better code
    • peer review finds bugs
    • fast turn-around for fixes
  • Active developer community
    • superb technical support
    • rapid evolution/improvement
    • no orphan products -- someone can always take over
  • Active user community
    • mutual assistance
    • close relationship with developers
    • widespread expertise
